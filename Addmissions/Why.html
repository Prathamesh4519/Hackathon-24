Why IIITN??

1. Academics & Courses IIITN offers B.Tech programs in:
✅ Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) Focus on AI, ML, Data Science, Cybersecurity, etc.
✅ Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) Covers IoT, VLSI, Embedded Systems, etc.
The institute follows a rigorous curriculum designed to match industry needs, ensuring that students are well-prepared for tech-driven careers.
2. Placements & Internship Opportunities Top Recruiters
: TCS, Infosys, L&T, Capgemini, Deloitte, and other well-known companies.
Average Package (recent years): Around 7-8 LPA (can vary). Highest Package: Can go above 20 LPA, depending on the student’s skills and opportunities.
Many students secure internships at top firms, which often convert into full-time offers.
3. Research & Projects
IIITN is involved in emerging technologies like AI, IoT, Data Science, and Cybersecurity.
Students can work on industry projects, research papers, and hackathons.
Collaboration opportunities with other IIITs, IITs, and research institutes.
4. Campus & Facilities
IIIT Nagpur has a modern campus with well-equipped labs, hostels, Wi-Fi, and classrooms.
Good infrastructure for coding, electronics research, and technical events.
Coding culture is growing, with students participating in GSoC, hackathons, and competitive programming.
5. Campus Life & Extracurriculars
Active coding clubs, technical societies, and cultural events.
Students participate in Hackathons, AI/ML Competitions, and Open Source Programs.
Annual fests, sports activities, and student organizations keep the campus lively.
6. Government-Backed Institute
IIIT Nagpur is an Institute of National Importance (INI), ensuring quality education and funding.
Being part of the IIIT ecosystem means better faculty, government support, and recognition.
